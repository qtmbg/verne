The Origin

A Gift of Meaning

Your friend, Jin Wang, came to Verne with the intention of creating something deeply personal, a piece that could hold meaning beyond form.

Shaped by a sense of love and friendship, this creation reflects her wish to honor you, your strength, your journey, and the resilience you carry.

Inspiration

Where It Began

“The design draws from the oak,
a symbol of resilience rooted beneath the surface.”

Like the roots of a tree, true strength is not always seen, yet it is what allows everything else to stand. In this piece, the oak is not used as decoration, but as a guiding structure, a reflection of balance, steadiness, and the kind of strength that does not need to announce itself.
